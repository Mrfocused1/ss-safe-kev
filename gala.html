The Purpose

More Than Just a
Masquerade.

Too often, the narrative around Sickle Cell Disease is one of pain and limitation. While we acknowledge the struggle, tonight we choose to focus on the power.

This masquerade ball is a symbol of unveiling the hidden strength of our community. We are gathering to honour the resilience of warriors, the dedication of carers, and the future we are building together through advocacy and innovation.
