Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

SickleSafe is a health management application designed for people living with sickle cell disease. We built SickleSafe with your privacy as a first principle.

All personal data you enter into SickleSafe is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not operate servers that store your personal health information, and we do not transmit your data to any remote server operated by SickleSafe. Your health data belongs to you and stays on your device.

Data We Collect

The following information may be stored locally on your device when you use SickleSafe:

  • Your name and date of birth (used to personalise your Medical ID)
  • Health logs — including pain levels, hydration records, and medication tracking
  • Notification preferences
  • Community posts and messages you choose to share within the app (note: community content is transmitted via community infrastructure so it can be received by other users — your health logs and Medical ID remain on-device only and are never transmitted)

All personal health data (health logs, Medical ID, notification preferences) is stored exclusively via AsyncStorage on your device. It is not uploaded to or accessible from any SickleSafe server.

SOS & Circle of Care Notifications

When you activate the SOS feature or send a message to your Circle of Care, a notification is delivered to your contacts via Apple Push Notification Service (APNs). No health data or location data is included in these notifications.

Data We Do Not Collect

SickleSafe does not collect or store the following:

  • Location data — the app does not access or record your location
  • Contacts — the app does not access your contacts list
  • Calendar data — the app does not read from or write to your calendar
  • Biometric data — Face ID or Touch ID may be used to unlock your Medical ID in-session as an authentication mechanism only. The result of biometric authentication is never stored or transmitted

Third-Party Services

SickleSafe makes read-only requests to the following public APIs to provide educational content within the app:

  • ClinicalTrials.gov API — to display publicly available clinical trial information relevant to sickle cell disease
  • OpenFDA API — to display publicly available medication information

No personal data is sent to either of these services. All requests are read-only and fetch publicly available information only.

Camera & Microphone

SickleSafe may request access to your camera and microphone for the following limited purposes:

  • Attaching photos to community messages or posts
  • Scanning medication barcodes for quick medication entry

Media captured within the app is stored locally on your device only and is not uploaded to any server.

Children

SickleSafe is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used SickleSafe, please contact us at privacy@sicklesafe.co.uk.

Your Rights

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your device settings.

If you would like to request deletion of your account and any associated data held by SickleSafe (for example, any community content), please email support@sicklesafe.co.uk with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will action your request within 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact:

privacy@sicklesafe.co.uk
